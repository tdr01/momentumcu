Giving Back To Local Kids In Need

We’re thrilled to share our Holiday Toy Drive is back for its third year! Our branch staff is once again collecting toys for kids aged 12 and under which will be provided to organizations in each of our branch communities.

With the hustle and bustle of the holiday season, it’s easy to forget that not every child gets to experience the same joy on Christmas morning. By providing toys to kids in need, we are also helping alleviate stress for parents who may not be able to afford to buy their kids gifts themselves.

For those who want to help but don’t have time to shop, we’re also collecting monetary donations to our toy drive fund which will be used to purchase additional toys.

This year, toys will be donated to:

  • Brampton – Honeychurch Family Life Resource Centre
  • Oakville – Halton Women’s Place
  • Hamilton – CityKidz

Thanks to our staff, members, and partners, we’ve been able to make the holiday season a little brighter for hundreds of kids in need over the years and we look forward to doing it again! We couldn’t be more grateful for your support.

Donations will be accepted until December 18th.
