Consolidation Loan

Home / Personal Services / Loans / Consolidation Loan

If you are having difficulty maintaining your debt, this product might be the right fit for you. We understand that owing a few dollars here and there is pretty normal these days, but what we don’t understand is charging you a ridiculous amount of interest. Let us help you pay off those unwanted high interest debts and provide you with one affordable payment to help you get your finances in order.

Features:

  • Term loans available from 1 – 6 years (secured & unsecured)
  • Competitive variable or fixed rates
  • Choice of repayment schedule
  • Quick pre-approvals
  • Option to make extra payments or pay down at any time without penalty
  • Option to include life, disability and critical illness insurance

Contact the Solution Centre to apply or fill out our online application.
