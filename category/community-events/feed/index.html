The MCU team is thrilled to be supporting the Down Syndrome Association of Hamilton (DSAH) again this year by sponsoring The WALK on Sunday, September 30th.

Formed in 1982, DSAH provides support to local families, works to educate the community, and encourages community programs that help enrich the lives of those living with Down syndrome. DSAH hosts The WALK annually in support of the GO21 national awareness campaign hosted by the Canadian Down Syndrome Society. The event aims to increase awareness, understanding, and acceptance of Down syndrome within the community.

The WALK is taking place at Bayfront Park from 8:30am to 12:00pm. In addition to supporting a great cause, there will be raffles, live music, and fun activities for kids! The MCU team will be on-site and we encourage you to join us for this fun-filled day by registering a team, volunteering to help, or donating.

Visit the Down Syndrome Association of Hamilton’s website for more information.

We’re helping support youth in need this year by supporting the Ontario Credit Union Foundation’s CU Succeed Bursary program!

Each year, 30% of youth in Ontario choose not to pursue higher education because of financial barriers and OCUF is working to change that. To date, the CU Succeed Bursary program has provided $111,000 to help students pursue their dreams of post-secondary education.

The 2018 goal is to raise $75,000 and MCU will be supporting the campaign by hosting various fundraising activities throughout the month of May and by accepting donations to the fund online. Members can donate to the fund by visiting our team page here. Tax receipts will be provided.

We will also be holding an in-branch raffle from May 7th to May 18th with all proceeds being donated to the bursary program. Visit a branch to enter for a chance to score a great prize!

Do you know someone looking for student funding?

Applications to the CU Succeed Youth Bursary are being accepted until June 30, 2018. Click here for more information about eligibility and how to apply.

Thanks for helping us support this very important initiative!

With the hustle and bustle of the holiday season, it’s easy to forget that not every child gets to experience the same joy on Christmas morning. By providing toys to kids in need, we are also helping alleviate stress for parents who may not be able to afford to buy their kids gifts themselves.

For those who want to help but don’t have time to shop, we’re also collecting monetary donations to our toy drive fund which will be used to purchase additional toys.

This year, toys will be donated to:

  • Brampton – Honeychurch Family Life Resource Centre
  • Oakville – Halton Women’s Place
  • Hamilton – CityKidz

International Credit Union (ICU) Day® is taking place a week from today so it seemed only fitting to share more info about this very special celebration!

Credit unions have been celebrating what makes us different (and better!) on ICU Day® since 1948. The day is held to recognize the history and achievements of CUs around the world while raising awareness about all the great work they are doing. Credit unions everywhere will celebrate in various ways including fundraisers, giveaways, contests, and open houses.

This year, we are highlighting what makes credit unions truly unique under the theme of “The Authentic Difference” launched by the World Council of Credit Unions that works as an advocate for credit unions in matters of legislation, education and training, networking, outreach and encouraging the co-operative model.

It’s a little known fact that credit unions have played an important role in the Canadian financial industry. With over 100 years serving its members, credit unions are responsible for many important “firsts”. Some of them are:

• First to provide ownership and membership to all members

• First personal loan based on borrower’s character

• First payroll deduction service for deposits and loan payments

• First open mortgages

• First to lend to women in their own names

• First variable rate mortgage

• First personal line of credit

• First full-service ATM network

• First home equity line of credit

• First debit card service

• First point-of-sale debit payment service

• First registered education savings plan

• First fully-functional online banking

We know—credit unions are pretty awesome!

We haven’t forgotten our members either. We will be handing out a special gift to show appreciation to all members who stop in to see us on October 20th.

Be sure to check out #ICUDay on social media next week to see what all the credit unions are up to on ICU Day®!
