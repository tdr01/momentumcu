International Credit Union Day is on October 18th this year and we’ll be celebrating its 70th anniversary!

Since 1948, credit unions around the world have celebrated the credit union movement on the 3rd Thursday of October. It’s the perfect day to highlight credit union achievements, to spread awareness about the great things credit unions are doing, and to thank our members for their years of commitment.

This year’s theme is “Find your Platinum Lining in Credit Unions” and we hope all of our members have found theirs with MCU!

Member who stop by a branch on October 18th will have the chance to win a $150 gift card prize pack and can enjoy some delicious treats with our team. We encourage you to share your credit union stories on social media using #ICUDay.

For more information about ICU Day visit the World Council of Credit Unions’ website. We look forward to celebrating with you on International Credit Union Day!

This is a reminder to Credit Union Mastercard cardholders that the relationship between MCU and CUETS Financial, our Mastercard provider, ended on July 31st, 2018. This change will be seamless for most CUETS Financial credit card cardholders and no action is required as you can continue to use existing credit cards the same way you do now.

Important Change to Note:

MCU no longer offers CUETS Financial cards or is able to provide support in our branches for CUETS Financial credit cards. To continue managing your account online, you can access your account online using your current login credentials for CUETS Financial Online Banking. If you aren’t currently enrolled, you can visit cucardsonline.com and click the “New User/Enrol Now” link. You can also contact CUETS Financial toll-free at 1-800-561-7849.

All cardholders should have already been contacted by CUETS Financial in advance of this change. Be sure to bookmark or add the following useful links to your favorites so you can access this information when you need it:

www.choicerewards.ca – For information about card features and benefits and to make redemptions using CHOICE REWARDS or Platinum Class points.

www.cucardsonline.com – Secure access to online card services, including recent transactions and monthly statements.

If you have any questions about this change, contact CUETS Financial directly at 1-800-561-7849.

Thank you for your cooperation with this important change.

SPECIAL RESOLUTION

The Board of Directors will also be asking the membership to consider a special resolution to amend the Credit Union’s by-laws to:

• reduce the size of the Board from 9 directors to 7 directors effective from and after the Credit Union’s AGM in 2019,

• remove the qualification that any director candidate have been a member of the Credit Union for at least one year before seeking election to the Board, and

• increase the term limit for directors from 3 consecutive 3-year terms (i.e., 9 consecutive years) to 5 consecutive 3-year terms (i.e., 15 consecutive years).

OUR NEW ONLINE BANKING SPENDING AND BUDGETING TOOLS ARE HERE!

Take everything you know about yourself financially and put it where you can see it all at once. What do you have? A bigger picture. We’ve added a new Personal Financial Management (PFM) tool to online banking to help you better understand your money. These smart new online banking features let you see and understand your money like never before. Our new spending and budgeting tools show you how you spend, save and live today so that you can make decisions that are right for you tomorrow.

Click here to see what’s new.

With our new online banking experience, you can:

• See your transactions automatically categorized and graphically displayed in the account summary page

• Change and/or exclude transaction categorizations from your spending analysis

• Create and manage budgets based on your spending habits

• See all your accounts in one place by linking accounts you hold at other financial institutions

• Add assets and liabilities (e.g. car, home, or even money under the mattress) so you can see a more complete picture of your finances

• Create account groups so that transaction history and spending analysis for related accounts can be viewed together

• View your spending and budgets using online banking through the mobile website and MyMCU mobile banking app

Log in to online banking to get started!

Not signed up for online banking yet? Contact us. See a preview of what your new online banking looks like here.

For more information, see the Personal Financial Management (PFM) Demo Video and FAQs.
